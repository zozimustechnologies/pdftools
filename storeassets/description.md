## Full Description

**PDF Tools** by Zozimus Technologies is a powerful, privacy-first PDF toolkit built right into your Microsoft Edge side panel. Everything happens locally on your device — your files are never uploaded to any server.

### 🔧 PDF Operations
- **Extract Pages** — Pull out specific pages by number or range
- **Merge PDFs** — Combine multiple PDF files into a single document
- **Split PDF** — Separate every page into its own file instantly
- **Rotate Pages** — Rotate 90°, 180°, or 270° in one click
- **Edit Metadata** — Update title, author, subject, keywords, and more

### 🔒 Security & Protection
- **Password Protect** — Encrypt with AES-256; set separate user and owner passwords
- **Granular Permissions** — Control printing, copying, editing, annotations, and form filling independently
- **Remove Password** — Decrypt any PDF you own the password to
- **Add Watermark** — Custom text, opacity, placement, font size, and colour — with live preview

### ✍️ Digital Signatures
- **Draw Your Signature** — Sign directly in the panel using mouse or touch
- **Signature Metadata** — Attach your name, reason, and page number
- **Timestamp Support** — Add an authoritative timestamp to every signature
- **Full Customisation** — Choose ink colour, border colour, background, roundness, and more

### 📋 Document Information
- **File Info Tab** — View page count, PDF version, file size, and encryption status at a glance
- **Permission Summary** — See exactly what operations the PDF allows

### ⚙️ Settings
- Auto-download processed PDFs
- Enable or disable in-panel notifications
- Language preference (English, Spanish, French)

### 🔒 Privacy & Security
- **Zero uploads** — All processing runs 100% in your browser using PDF-lib and PDF.js
- **No accounts required** — No sign-in, no tracking, no analytics
- **No external servers** — Your documents never leave your device
- **Open libraries** — Built on trusted open-source PDF libraries

### Why PDF Tools?
Most online PDF tools require you to upload your files to a remote server — putting your confidential documents at risk. PDF Tools processes everything locally in your browser, making it safe for sensitive business documents, legal files, and personal records.
